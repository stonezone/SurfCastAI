SUMMARY
Two main swell trains to watch: 1) North/NW groundswell from a developing gale/frontal complex north of Hawaii (998 mb + stronger Aleutian 974 mb system) — arrival window mid-late week, building to significant north-facing surf. 2) Shorter-period but locally significant south-southeast groundswell from Tropical Storm Halong (close, ~12N/150W) — arrives sooner (24-48 hrs). Tropical Storm Octave (near 125W) is farther east and will be a secondary south-southwest groundswell source later in the 4–6 day window. Trades/highs east of the islands will modulate surf quality (onshore/chop for many exposures).

- Tropical Storm Octave (secondary tropical source)
